About me

I am a Software Engineer from Kyzylorda, Kazakhstan, specializing in building Windows applications using C# and WPF. With over 5 years of experience, I develop desktop applications, widgets, and tools that make working on a computer more convenient and engaging.

I enjoy turning ideas into working software and delving into the field of cybersecurity. I not only create useful software but also explore ethical hacking, work with Kali Linux, and constantly expand my knowledge in this area.

In my work, I use modern tools such as Visual Studio, Figma, After Effects, GitHub, Unreal Engine, Firebase, and more. I am proud of projects like BIBON (a system optimization tool for Windows) and Tagbir (a desktop widget).

My goal is to create functional, user-friendly, and secure solutions that bring real value to users. I am always open to new ideas and strive to grow as a specialist.
